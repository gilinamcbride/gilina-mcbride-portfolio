About Me

Gilina is a current student of UNC Chapel Hill Coding Bootcamp, and an alumna of UNC Chapel Hill. She graduated in 2015 with a degree in Journalism and Mass Communication (Public Relations specialization) and Political Science. Gilina has worked for several years in in-house marketing with and emphasis on social media, graphic design, content management, and website management. Most recently, her work has been in the disaster restoration field working as an estimator and project manager. Her background in marketing and design lends to an effortless transition into web development, and she hopes to pursue a career in full stack development.
